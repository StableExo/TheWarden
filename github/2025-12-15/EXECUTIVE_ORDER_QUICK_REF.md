# Executive Order: National Energy Security - Quick Reference

## Document Location

## Executive Summary

This executive order establishes the legal and operational framework for creating the **United States Energy Corporation (USEC)** as outlined in the US_DEBT_2027_AUTONOMOUS_PLAN.

### Key Provisions

#### 1. **Creation of USEC**
- Federally chartered public-private corporation
- 15-member Board of Directors (Presidential, gubernatorial, and stakeholder appointments)
- $500 billion initial federal capitalization
- Mission: National energy grid operator with AI optimization

#### 2. **Infrastructure Acquisition**
- Fair market buyout of existing grid infrastructure
- 6-month timeline for majority acquisition
- Multiple compensation options (cash, equity, hybrid)
- Eminent domain only as last resort (125% compensation)

#### 3. **Three-Phase Implementation**

**Phase 1 (Months 1-6): Foundation**
- Establish USEC structure
- Deploy AI systems in major cities
- Complete infrastructure acquisition
- Launch cybersecurity operations

**Phase 2 (Months 7-12): Optimization**
- National AI deployment
- Achieve efficiency gains
- Begin rate reductions
- Initiate nuclear projects

**Phase 3 (Months 13-24): Revenue Generation**
- 30% consumer cost reduction
- $1.5T annual revenue target
- Eliminate transmission losses
- Scale nuclear buildout

#### 4. **AI-Driven Optimization**
- Real-time demand prediction (99%+ accuracy)
- Smart grid load balancing
- Renewable energy integration
- Predictive maintenance
- Cybersecurity threat detection

#### 5. **Revenue Generation**
- **Target**: $1.5 trillion/year (ramping to $2T by 2027)
- Efficiency gains: $200B
- Export sales: $300B
- Consumer savings capture: $400B
- Nuclear expansion: $500B

#### 6. **Revenue Allocation**
- 75% to national debt reduction ($1.125T)
- 15% to infrastructure reinvestment ($225B)
- 5% to R&D ($75B)
- 5% to consumer assistance ($75B)

#### 7. **Consumer Protection**
- 30% rate reduction within 24 months
- No disconnections during extreme weather
- Assistance for low-income households
- 99.9% uptime guarantee

#### 8. **National Security**
- Unified grid management
- 24/7 cybersecurity operations
- DHS and DoD coordination
- Supply chain security (US preference)

#### 9. **Oversight & Accountability**
- FERC rate-setting authority
- DOE technical support
- Congressional quarterly reports
- Public transparency dashboard
- Independent GAO audits

#### 10. **Worker & Environmental Protections**
- Job guarantees for existing utility workers
- Collective bargaining rights
- 50% renewable by Year 5
- 100% carbon-free by 2035

## Legal Authority

- National Emergencies Act
- Defense Production Act
- Federal Power Act
- Energy Policy Act
- Infrastructure Investment and Jobs Act
