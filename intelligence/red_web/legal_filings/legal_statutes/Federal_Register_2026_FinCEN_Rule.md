## Key Provisions

### Expanded Statutes (NEW)
Now covers violations of:
- **BSA** (Bank Secrecy Act) — original coverage
- **IEEPA** (International Emergency Economic Powers Act) ← **NK/sanctions violations**
- **TWEA** (Trading With the Enemy Act)
- **Kingpin Act** (narco/sanctions designations)
- Conspiracies to violate any of the above

**Red Web relevance: North Korea (TraderTraitor) overlap = IEEPA violation = covered ✅**

### Mandatory Minimum Award
- **10% minimum MANDATORY** — FinCEN MUST pay at least this
- **30% maximum**
- **Auto-max rule:** If 30% of sanctions ≤ $15M → you get full 30% automatically

### Aggregation Rule
FinCEN ($500K) + OFAC ($500K) + DOJ ($500K) = $1.5M aggregate → qualifies as single covered action

### Financial Integrity Fund (Self-Funded)
Awards paid from revolving fund — no Congressional appropriation wait.
