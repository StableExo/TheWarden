## In Action: Trade Failure Response

When a trade fails, TheWarden responds at ALL levels simultaneously:

- **L0**: Trade fails
- **L1**: "My slippage calculation was wrong"
- **L2**: "Third time underestimating on Aerodrome DEX"
- **L3**: "I'm using historical average instead of DEX-specific analysis"
- **L4**: "Why do I treat all DEXs uniformly when they differ?"
- **L5**: "My slippage accuracy degraded after adding new DEXs"
- **L6**: "Do I 'learn' from failures or just update parameters?"
