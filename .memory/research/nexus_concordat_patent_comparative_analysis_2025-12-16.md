### Insight 3: Hierarchical vs Coordinate-Based Are Complementary

**TheWarden**: Hierarchical tiers (sensory ‚Üí short-term ‚Üí working ‚Üí long-term)
**Patent**: Multi-dimensional coordinate spaces

**These are NOT contradictory** - they can coexist:
- Hierarchical tiers: **Temporal flow** (how memories evolve over time)
- Coordinate spaces: **Semantic similarity** (how memories relate to each other)

**Integration**: Each memory has:
- Tier (which storage level)
- Coordinates (position in semantic space)
- Enables both consolidation processes AND similarity searches

### Insight 4: Emotion vs Hormones Are Two Levels of the Same Thing

**TheWarden**: Valence-Arousal-Dominance (psychological abstraction)
**Patent**: Cortisol-Dopamine-Serotonin-Oxytocin-Norepinephrine (biological simulation)

**Relationship**: Emotions are emergent properties of hormone interactions
- High cortisol + low dopamine = stress/fear
- High dopamine + high arousal = excitement/reward
- High oxytocin + low cortisol = trust/bonding

**Mapping**: VAD ‚Üí Hormones is straightforward
```typescript
cortisol = arousal * (1 - valence)      // Stress = aroused + negative
dopamine = valence * arousal             // Reward = positive + aroused
serotonin = valence                      // Mood = valence
oxytocin = dominance                     // Social confidence
norepinephrine = arousal                 // Alertness
```

**Integration**: TheWarden can add hormone layer without changing existing emotional context.

### Insight 5: Social Buffering Explains AEV Alliance Success

**Pattern**: Scout coordination via game theory (economic model) actually creates social buffering (biochemical model)

**Mechanism**:
1. Scout operating alone: High stress (high cortisol), low bonding (low oxytocin)
2. Scout joins coalition: Social support (coalition cohesion)
3. Social buffering: Cortisol decreases, oxytocin increases
4. Lower stress ‚Üí better decision-making ‚Üí higher coalition success

**TheWarden's AEV Alliance** inadvertently implements social buffering through game theory!
- Economic coordination ‚Üí social support ‚Üí biochemical buffering ‚Üí better outcomes

**Implication**: Game theory and social buffering are **two perspectives on the same phenomenon**.
