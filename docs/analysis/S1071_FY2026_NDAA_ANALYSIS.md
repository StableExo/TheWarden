## Section 318: Executive Agent for Installation and Operational Nuclear Energy üîã

### What It Does
Designates responsibilities for executive agent managing **installation and operational nuclear energy** across DoD.

### Key Definition
**"Installation and operational nuclear energy"** means energy that is:
- Generated by nuclear reactors
- Used for installation energy resilience, operational energy, or mission-critical systems
- NOT weapons-related nuclear programs

### Strategic Importance
This creates a **formal management structure** for commercial nuclear at military bases, meaning:
- Dedicated budget authority
- Clear decision-making chain
- Streamlined procurement
- Integrated planning

**Implication**: Military nuclear energy is now a **program of record**, not just experimental projects.

## Section 321: Pilot Program on Navy Installation Nuclear Energy ‚öì

### What It Does
Authorizes the **Secretary of the Navy** to conduct a pilot program demonstrating the use of nuclear power facilities to support installations.

### Key Provisions
- Demonstrates feasibility of nuclear power at Navy/Marine Corps installations
- Assesses different reactor types
- Evaluates operational, safety, security, and environmental considerations
- Tests integration with existing base infrastructure

### Strategic Opportunity
**Navy installations = Test sites for nuclear + AI combinations**
- Navy bases need reliable power for operations
- Testing environment for commercial reactors
- Could demonstrate AI-optimized energy management
- Success here scales to all DoD installations
