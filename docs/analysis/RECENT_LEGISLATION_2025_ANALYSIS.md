**Regulatory Jurisdiction**:
- **CFTC (Commodity Futures Trading Commission)**: Exclusive jurisdiction for digital commodity spot markets
- **SEC (Securities and Exchange Commission)**: Oversight of investment contract assets (securities)

- **Compliance standards** for custody, reporting, recordkeeping

- Assets that are primarily used as means of exchange or stores of value
- Not securities under Howey Test
- Includes Bitcoin, Ethereum (under certain conditions), and others

3. **Annual Reporting** - Progress tracking and accountability
4. **Critical Infrastructure** - Guidance for private sector (finance, healthcare, energy, etc.)
5. **NIST Standards** - Adopt post-quantum cryptography algorithms
6. **Performance Benchmarks** - Measurable milestones for migration

**Agencies Involved**:
- White House Office of Science and Technology Policy (OSTP)
- Subcommittee on Economic and Security Implications of Quantum Information Science (ESIX)

**Timeline Requirements**:
- **2027**: Pilot systems migrated to post-quantum crypto
